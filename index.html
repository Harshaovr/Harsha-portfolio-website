PROJECTS

ELDERLY CARE AND VIRTUAL SOCIAL INTERACTION

In today's digital era, elderly individuals, especially those living alone, face significant challenges like isolation and lack of timely healthcare. Our project addresses these issues by introducing an Elderly Care and Social Interaction platform powered by deep learning. This system monitors health metrics (heart rate, activity level) and environmental data (motion events) in real-time, sending alerts to caregivers during emergencies. Additionally, it offers virtual interaction through video calls, voice messages, and chat, and reminds users to take medications and consult physicians on time. By combining technology and compassion, we aim to enhance the quality of life for the elderly.

AI VOICE CONTROLLED CHATBOT

Chatbots are AI systems that simulate human conversation using AI and Natural Language Processing (NLP). They are widely used in sectors like education, healthcare, and business, communicating via text or voice. ChatGPT, developed by OpenAI, is a notable example. In 2023, AI chatbots and voicebots will expand into fields such as insurance, banking, retail, and healthcare. This project aims to implement speech recognition in Python, enabling a voice-based chatbot.

ROUTE MAP and DATA SHARE SYSTEM

The Route Map and Personal Data Share app uses GPS to track user locations and share personal data via QR codes. When users leave home, they can store their location in a database with a single click. At public places, users can send their personal data to contacts through the app. A key feature of our project is the security of user data; it is securely stored in the database and cannot be accessed without the user's permission. This ensures that user information remains private and protected while providing convenient data sharing capabilities.
